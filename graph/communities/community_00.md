# Community 0 — Contact Center Transformation

## Theme

This community clusters every Contact Center Transformation RFP in the corpus. The connecting thread is the modernisation of customer-contact operations — replacing legacy call-handling with integrated, omnichannel service platforms — for two very different buyers: a public-sector body and an aerospace group.

## Budget Profile

Across the 10 RFPs in this cluster, indicative budgets range from **USD 440,367** to **USD 2,017,705**, with an average midpoint of roughly **USD 1,152,980** per engagement. Each RFP states its own budget range and asks vendors to separate one-time implementation costs from recurring support fees.

The scope-area concepts (e.g. *Data migration and validation*, *Discovery and current-state assessment*, *Implementation and configuration*) and the strategic-driver concepts (e.g. *Process Standardization*, *Compliance & Audit Readiness*) are shared hub nodes that link this community to all other service-line communities. Notably, *Contact Center Transformation* shares its scope vocabulary and several drivers with the Enterprise Software Platform (Community 1), Cloud Migration Services (Community 3) and ERP Implementation (Community 7) clusters, meaning a vendor strong in those shared capabilities can address demand across multiple service lines.

## Strategic Significance

Signals capability in CX operations modernisation, telephony/omnichannel platform delivery, and managed contact-centre run services. The public-sector and aerospace mix shows demand spans regulated, service-citizen environments and complex industrial organisations.
