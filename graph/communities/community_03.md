# Community 3 — Cloud Migration Services

## Theme

This community clusters all Cloud Migration Services RFPs — moving workloads and data to cloud with emphasis on implementation, configuration and change management. Buyers are a financial-services group and a pharmaceuticals group, both in heavily regulated sectors.

- **Service:** Cloud Migration Services
- **Clients:** Financial Services Holdings, Pharmaceuticals Holdings
- **Strategic drivers (concept):** Customer & Employee Experience, Data Visibility for Leadership, Operational Efficiency, Process Standardization, Time-to-Market Acceleration
- **Financial instruments:** 10 per-RFP indicative budgets (USD)

## Budget Profile

Across the 10 RFPs in this cluster, indicative budgets range from **USD 744,423** to **USD 2,327,520**, with an average midpoint of roughly **USD 1,275,364** per engagement. Each RFP states its own budget range and asks vendors to separate one-time implementation costs from recurring support fees.

## Scope Coverage

RFPs in this community draw scope areas from a common pool; across the cluster the following are requested:

- Data migration and validation
- Discovery and current-state assessment
- Implementation and configuration
- Managed services and post-launch support
- Reporting, analytics, and governance
- Solution design and architecture
- Testing and cutover support
- Training, adoption, and change management

## Strategic Drivers by Client

- **Financial Services Holdings:** Customer & Employee Experience, Operational Efficiency, Process Standardization, Time-to-Market Acceleration
- **Pharmaceuticals Holdings:** Customer & Employee Experience, Data Visibility for Leadership, Operational Efficiency

## Cross-community Connections

The scope-area concepts (e.g. *Data migration and validation*, *Discovery and current-state assessment*, *Implementation and configuration*) and the strategic-driver concepts (e.g. *Process Standardization*, *Compliance & Audit Readiness*) are shared hub nodes that link this community to all other service-line communities. Notably, *Cloud Migration Services* shares its scope vocabulary and several drivers with the Enterprise Software Platform (Community 1), Cloud Migration Services (Community 3) and ERP Implementation (Community 7) clusters, meaning a vendor strong in those shared capabilities can address demand across multiple service lines.

## Strategic Significance

Signals capability in cloud landing-zone design, workload migration and adoption/change management for regulated industries. Budgets are tightly banded with a high floor, indicating consistently substantial engagements.
