## Budget Profile

Across the 10 RFPs in this cluster, indicative budgets range from **USD 448,016** to **USD 2,574,608**, with an average midpoint of roughly **USD 1,187,041** per engagement. Each RFP states its own budget range and asks vendors to separate one-time implementation costs from recurring support fees.
