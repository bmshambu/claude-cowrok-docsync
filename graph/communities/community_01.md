# Community 1 — Enterprise Software Platform

## Theme

This community groups all Enterprise Software Platform RFPs — large, cross-functional platform programmes intended to standardise processes across business units. The buyers are a healthcare operator and a hospitality group, both replacing a mix of legacy tools and manual processes.

- **Service:** Enterprise Software Platform
- **Clients:** Healthcare Holdings, Hospitality Holdings
- **Strategic drivers (concept):** Compliance & Audit Readiness, Customer & Employee Experience, Operational Efficiency, Process Standardization, Technology Risk & Technical Debt Reduction, Time-to-Market Acceleration, Total Cost of Ownership Reduction
- **Financial instruments:** 10 per-RFP indicative budgets (USD)

## Budget Profile

Across the 10 RFPs in this cluster, indicative budgets range from **USD 379,082** to **USD 3,393,666**, with an average midpoint of roughly **USD 1,564,459** per engagement. Each RFP states its own budget range and asks vendors to separate one-time implementation costs from recurring support fees.

## Scope Coverage

RFPs in this community draw scope areas from a common pool; across the cluster the following are requested:

- Data migration and validation
- Discovery and current-state assessment
- Implementation and configuration
- Managed services and post-launch support
- Reporting, analytics, and governance
- Solution design and architecture
- Testing and cutover support
- Training, adoption, and change management

## Strategic Drivers by Client

- **Healthcare Holdings:** Customer & Employee Experience, Process Standardization, Technology Risk & Technical Debt Reduction, Time-to-Market Acceleration
- **Hospitality Holdings:** Compliance & Audit Readiness, Operational Efficiency, Process Standardization, Total Cost of Ownership Reduction

## Cross-community Connections

The scope-area concepts (e.g. *Data migration and validation*, *Discovery and current-state assessment*, *Implementation and configuration*) and the strategic-driver concepts (e.g. *Process Standardization*, *Compliance & Audit Readiness*) are shared hub nodes that link this community to all other service-line communities. Notably, *Enterprise Software Platform* shares its scope vocabulary and several drivers with the Enterprise Software Platform (Community 1), Cloud Migration Services (Community 3) and ERP Implementation (Community 7) clusters, meaning a vendor strong in those shared capabilities can address demand across multiple service lines.

## Strategic Significance

Signals capability in core platform selection, design and rollout at enterprise scale. This is the highest-budget cluster in the corpus, indicating appetite for major transformation programmes.
