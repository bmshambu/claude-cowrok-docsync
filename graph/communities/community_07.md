# Community 7 — ERP Implementation

## Theme

This community groups all ERP Implementation RFPs — core enterprise resource planning programmes with heavy emphasis on data migration and validation. Buyers are an energy group and a logistics group seeking process standardisation and leadership visibility.

- **Service:** ERP Implementation
- **Clients:** Energy Holdings, Logistics Holdings

## Budget Profile

Across the 10 RFPs in this cluster, indicative budgets range from **USD 321,669** to **USD 2,817,111**, with an average midpoint of roughly **USD 1,467,197** per engagement. Each RFP states its own budget range and asks vendors to separate one-time implementation costs from recurring support fees.

## Scope Coverage

RFPs in this community draw scope areas from a common pool; across the cluster the following are requested:

- Data migration and validation
- Discovery and current-state assessment
- Implementation and configuration
- Managed services and post-launch support
- Reporting, analytics, and governance
- Solution design and architecture
- Testing and cutover support
- Training, adoption, and change management

## Cross-community Connections

The scope-area concepts (e.g. *Data migration and validation*, *Discovery and current-state assessment*, *Implementation and configuration*) and the strategic-driver concepts (e.g. *Process Standardization*, *Compliance & Audit Readiness*) are shared hub nodes that link this community to all other service-line communities. Notably, *ERP Implementation* shares its scope vocabulary and several drivers with the Enterprise Software Platform (Community 1), Cloud Migration Services (Community 3) and ERP Implementation (Community 7) clusters, meaning a vendor strong in those shared capabilities can address demand across multiple service lines.

## Strategic Significance

Signals capability in ERP design, build and data migration for asset- and operations-intensive industries. Among the higher-budget clusters, reflecting programme scale.
