# Community 4 — Managed Security Services

## Theme

This community groups Managed Security Services RFPs — ongoing security operations, monitoring and risk reduction. Buyers are a technology firm and a retail group, both prioritising technology-risk and technical-debt reduction.

## Budget Profile

Across the 10 RFPs in this cluster, indicative budgets range from **USD 246,823** to **USD 1,909,867**, with an average midpoint of roughly **USD 925,300** per engagement. Each RFP states its own budget range and asks vendors to separate one-time implementation costs from recurring support fees.

## Scope Coverage

RFPs in this community draw scope areas from a common pool; across the cluster the following are requested:

- Data migration and validation
- Discovery and current-state assessment
- Implementation and configuration
- Managed services and post-launch support
- Reporting, analytics, and governance
- Solution design and architecture
- Testing and cutover support
- Training, adoption, and change management

## Strategic Drivers by Client

- **Retail Holdings:** Customer & Employee Experience, Process Standardization, Technology Risk & Technical Debt Reduction
- **Technology Holdings:** Compliance & Audit Readiness, Customer & Employee Experience, Technology Risk & Technical Debt Reduction, Total Cost of Ownership Reduction

## Cross-community Connections

The scope-area concepts (e.g. *Data migration and validation*, *Discovery and current-state assessment*, *Implementation and configuration*) and the strategic-driver concepts (e.g. *Process Standardization*, *Compliance & Audit Readiness*) are shared hub nodes that link this community to all other service-line communities. Notably, *Managed Security Services* shares its scope vocabulary and several drivers with the Enterprise Software Platform (Community 1), Cloud Migration Services (Community 3) and ERP Implementation (Community 7) clusters, meaning a vendor strong in those shared capabilities can address demand across multiple service lines.

## Strategic Significance

Signals capability in security operations, monitoring and managed detection/response. This is the lowest-budget cluster, consistent with recurring run-services rather than large build programmes.
